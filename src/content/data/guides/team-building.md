# Team Building Fundamentals

Learn how to build effective teams for different match types.

## Understanding Stats

### POW (Power)
- Affects spike and attack strength
- Important for offensive plays

### TEC (Technique)
- Affects accuracy and skill activation
- Important for consistent performance

### STM (Stamina)
- Affects endurance during matches
- Important for longer events

### APL (Appeal)
- Affects scoring in gravure and appeal matches
- Important for festival events

## Team Composition

### Balanced Team
- Mix of high POW and TEC characters
- Good for general content

### Offensive Team
- Focus on POW stats
- Best for quick matches

### Endurance Team
- Focus on STM stats
- Best for marathon events

## Swimsuit Selection

### Matching Character to Swimsuit
- Each character has base stats
- Swimsuits add bonus stats
- Match swimsuit strengths to character strengths

### Skill Synergies
- Look for complementary skills
- Some skills boost team performance
- Event-specific skills are valuable

## Advanced Tips

1. Level up your main team first
2. Unlock potential for stat boosts
3. Upgrade accessories for extra stats
4. Use trend swimsuits during events
