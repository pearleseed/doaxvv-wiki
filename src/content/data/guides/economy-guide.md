# V-Stone Economy Guide

Master the art of V-Stone management to maximize your gacha success.

## V-Stone Sources

### Daily Sources
- Daily missions: ~50-100 V-Stones
- Login bonus: varies by day
- Event participation: 100-500+ V-Stones

### Weekly Sources
- Weekly missions: ~200-300 V-Stones
- Ranking rewards: varies by placement

### One-Time Sources
- Story completion
- Achievement milestones
- New girl episodes

## Spending Strategies

### When to Pull
- Limited/Collaboration banners with must-have swimsuits
- Anniversary and special celebration banners
- Step-up banners with guaranteed SSR

### When to Save
- Regular permanent banners
- Reruns of swimsuits you don't need
- When close to pity on another banner

## Budget Planning

### Free-to-Play Monthly Budget
- Estimated monthly income: 8,000-12,000 V-Stones
- Plan for 1-2 pity cycles per month maximum

### Efficient Spending Tips
1. Never pull singles - always do 10-pulls
2. Track your pity counter
3. Set a budget before pulling
4. Stop at the first SSR if not targeting specific items
